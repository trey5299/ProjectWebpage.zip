About Us

We are an American association that is involved in the design, development, manufacturing and world wide marketing and sales of apparel, footwear, accessories, equipment and services. Our mission is to do everything humanly possible to expand human potential. We will accomplish that by creating amazing sports innovations, by making our products more substainably, by building a creative and divergent global team and making a positive impact in the community.
